<section class="info-grid">
  <article class="info-card">
    <p class="info-card__label">Research areas</p>
    <h2>Language models that are more dependable</h2>
    <p>My work spans LLM agents, reasoning and planning, long-context behavior, memory, multilingual modeling, robustness, and alignment. I am interested in systems that perform reliably outside narrow benchmark settings.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="info-card">
    <p class="info-card__label">Current focus</p>
    <h2>Practical evaluation and control for agentic systems</h2>
    <p>I care about the gap between what models can do in demos and what they can do in sustained deployment. That includes benchmarks, training algorithms, and evaluation methods that surface failure modes early.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="info-card">
    <p class="info-card__label">Background</p>
    <h2>Industry and research across multiple settings</h2>
    <p>My research has crossed academia and industry, with work published in venues including ACL, NAACL, EMNLP, ICML, ICLR, CVPR, COLING, and AISTATS.</p>
  </article>
